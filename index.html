Welcome to Beetje Blauw, a Barcelona-based design studio
born from a fusion of global creativity. Rooted in the rich heritage
of Cyprus and South Africa, and refined in the Netherlands,
we transform ordinary spaces into living canvases.

Our name, Beetje Blauw, 'A Little Blue' in Dutch, echoes our love for cyanotype,
a blue-tinted vintage photographic technique. We marry this with our diverse inspirations,
spanning traditional painting methods to cutting-edge new media,
crafting a unique aesthetic that’s distinctly ours.

We're thrilled to present our latest collection:
sophisticated wallpapers designed to redefine modern homes and businesses.
Each design encapsulates a narrative of culture, artistry, and contemporary flair,
offering more than decoration – it's a conversation starter, a statement piece.

Meet Elliot and Kayla, the creative minds behind Beetje Blauw.

Elliot, our South African, specialises in painting and new media.
His work encapsulates vibrant portraits and intricate scenes, masterfully
incorporating elements of machinery to challenge traditional artistic boundaries.

Kayla, originating from Cyprus, brings a versatile skill set. She navigates the realms
of painting, cyanotype, illustration, and print with refined precision,
each piece a testament to her deep-rooted love for diverse art forms.

Together, they embody Beetje Blauw, crafting clean, sophisticated designs
that transform the everyday into extraordinary.
